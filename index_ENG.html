My name is Jakub Jończyk. I am an engineer, currently working on running a strength/metallographic laboratory in the automotive industry. In my work, I carry out various tests, starting with the design stage to serial tests. I also create websites as Front-end and Back-end developer . In addition, I pass my knowledge on to younger generations as a programming trainer.

Internal programs

As part of running the laboratory in 2021, I launched an initiative under which I create small "internal programs". These are software, usually written in Python, VBA or specific machine software, that are designed to facilitate and automate repetitive and time-consuming operations. As part of this initiative, the following programs have been created:

• Automatic segregation of files into specific folders along with their creation

• Automatic control of the correctness of the heat treatment process

• Automatic feasibility check for strength tests
